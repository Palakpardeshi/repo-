Our Story

Coffee Corner began in 2015 with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment. Our founders, two coffee enthusiasts who traveled the world tasting coffee, wanted to bring that global experience to our community.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms and roast them in small batches to ensure maximum freshness and flavor. Our pastries are baked fresh daily using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Whether you're stopping by for your morning espresso or having pastries delivered for a special occasion, we're committed to making your experience memorable.
